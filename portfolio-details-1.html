Details

In this project, I led the team of four people to dive into the statistics and datasets of TUIK to make an inference about the crime fact and poorness of Turkey in different perspectives such as crime rates investigation based on education, judgement, kids, poorness with respect to education, and race.

I had investigated many datasets and processed them with my friends to understand whether the different education level in Turkey causes different level of crime rates; gender based investigation made on the convicted people and detainees; kids' are important so that we have made an investigation for child crime with respect to children' gender, age group and also accustion of crime, victimization type and reason of arrival to police office; investigations are made to understand whether the education level affect wealth of people; also investigated the crime rates with respect to race as Turkey citizens and non-Turkey citizens.
